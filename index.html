Suggestions to Reduce Carbon Footprint

  • Consider using public transport, carpooling, or switching to an electric vehicle.
  • Use electricity from renewable sources like hydro or solar energy.
  • Reduce meat consumption, especially red meats like beef and lamb, and opt for plant-based meals.
  • Limit packed food and choose fresh, local produce to reduce packaging waste.
  • Optimize internet usage by downloading content instead of streaming.
  • Switch to energy-efficient appliances and light bulbs.
  • Recycle and reuse materials whenever possible.
  • Plant trees and support reforestation projects.
