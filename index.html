Use this calculator to estimate your carbon footprint and discover ways to reduce it.




Suggestions to Reduce Carbon Footprint

  • Consider using public transport or carpooling.
  • Switch to energy-efficient light bulbs.
  • Reduce meat consumption and opt for plant-based meals.
  • Recycle and reuse materials.
  • Plant trees and support reforestation projects.
